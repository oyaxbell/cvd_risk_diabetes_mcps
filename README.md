# Assessment of fatal cardiovascular disease risk using data-driven diabetes subgroups and SCORE2-Diabetes in 24,943 adults in Mexico City

Code and Supplementary data for paper: "Assessment of fatal cardiovascular disease risk using data-driven diabetes subgroups and SCORE2-Diabetes in 24,943 adults in Mexico City"
Preprint available at: https://www.medrxiv.org/content/10.64898/2025.12.15.25342299v1

DATA AVAILABILITY: Data from the Mexico City Prospective Study are available to bona fide researchers. For more details, the study’s Data and Sample Sharing policy may be downloaded (in English or Spanish) from https://www.ctsu.ox.ac.uk/research/mcps. Available study data can be examined in detail through the study’s Data Showcase, available at https://datashare.ndph.ox.ac.uk/mexico/.
